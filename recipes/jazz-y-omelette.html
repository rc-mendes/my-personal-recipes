jazzy-y omelette

a jazz-y omelette is an improvised omelette which you can make only with products you have at home. feel free to compose your healthy and conforting food to help you wake up or energize you up for the day.

ingredients

  • 4x eggs
  • somme butter
  • pepper&salt
  • fresh parsley
  • anything else you'd love to have in your omelette (my take would be to add some of the cheese I know you have in your frigo)

how to prepare

  1. add butter and a line of olive oil into a medium hot pan
  2. pour the eggs one by one when the pan is hot
  3. wait until the eggs start cooking
  4. you can add pepper, salt and some fresh parsley at this point
  5. mix everything to make it cook more even
  6. now you'll have to break the eggs into small pieces with a rubber spatula, always bringing to the edge of the pan cooked slices of the egg mixture
  7. just make sure it's all cooked and you can now serve
