everyday confort pasta

a very easy and cozy pasta you can rapidly prepare after going for some lazy shopping. don't forget to buy a nice red wine to make your lonely night perfect!

ingredients

  • 200g of pasta (I usually choose penne or fusilli)
  • salt&pepper
  • sauce bolognese or pesto or arriabatta or any other kind you would find at the supermarket

how to prepare

  1. put a big pot of water to boil
  2. when the water starts boiling, put your pasta for about the time it's adviced in the package of the pasta you broght
  3. in a pan you can slowly heat the pre-prepared sauce
  4. when the pasta is ready, you can mix a scoop of the water it boiled in with the sauce and add the pasta also
  5. let it sit for a minute out of the heat and you can serve with some more salt&pepper if you like
