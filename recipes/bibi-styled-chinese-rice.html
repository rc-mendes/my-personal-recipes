bibi styled chinese rice

this recipe can look tricky but it is very easy and I am sure you will cook it again and again. it's inspire in a tradition rice recipe that I don't really remember the name, but I learned it from a fellow chinese colleague from university and I adapted for a home-alone guys taste.

ingredients

  • 150g of rice
  • 2x eggs
  • butter
  • olive oil
  • salt&pepper
  • some cheese maybe
  • cube sliced vegetables like carots and potatoes
  • you can also add cherry tomatoes, peas, onion, etc.
  • a bit of chilli if you like as I do

how to prepare

  1. starting by adding in a medium hot pan 50g of butter and a fine line of olive oil
  2. add the rice (I suppose you have some rice at home from yesterday...)
  3. make two small holes in your ice to add the eggs
  4. let it cook for a bit and add slowly the rest of the ingredients starting with the more consistent vegetables
  5. add salt&pepper, the cheese and the chili - if you decided to
  6. mix everything gently toghether and everything is ready to be served
